History of TechCon

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the mission to unite professionals from across the tech industry to discuss, showcase, and explore new trends and innovations in technology. What started as a small gathering of enthusiasts has grown into a globally recognized conference, attracting attendees and speakers from all over the world.
